About me

Hello, I am Agustín Angulo (he/him) and I am a Computer Science major at University of California, Irvine (UCI). I just wrapped up my second year on campus and now I'm just waiting for my third year to start. The most important thing I am doing at UCI currently is acting as one of the board members for the UCI chapter of MAES, a club for Latinos in Science and Engineering. I am also a part of the SAGE Scholars program, which is a program at UCI focused on assisting low-income students with professional development and beginning their careers. These involvements stem from my passion to create spaces on campus and a sense of community for minoritized groups. Always feel free to reach out to me if you attend UCI or would like to attend, take care. :)
